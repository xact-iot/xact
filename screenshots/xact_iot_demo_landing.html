How to log in

  1. Open the demo URL in your browser:
    https://xact.dedyn.io/xact
  2. Enter the demo user credentials. Username demo and password demo.
  3. The demo user can view all dashboards and settings, but cannot edit or change anything.
  4. Select the dashboards on the left.
  5. Hover over icons on the maps to see more detail regarding the device under the pointer.
